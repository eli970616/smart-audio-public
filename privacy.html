MemoMind Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 13, 2026

Overview

MemoMind is a fully offline voice memo app. Recordings, transcripts, summaries, action items, and reminders are all created and stored on your device. No audio or generated text ever leaves your iPhone.

Data Collection

MemoMind does not collect personal data, audio recordings, transcripts, summaries, or action items. There is no MemoMind-operated server, and no third-party analytics, advertising, or telemetry SDKs are used.

On-Device Processing

Audio transcription and note generation use AI models that run locally on your iPhone. Your saved memos remain on your device unless you explicitly choose to share them using an iOS system feature such as the Share Sheet.

Network Access

MemoMind does not make network requests for its core features. The AI models needed for transcription and summarization are included with the app at install time, so the app works fully offline — including in Airplane Mode. The only network activity that may occur is what iOS itself performs on the app's behalf when you interact with the App Store, for example to start or restore a subscription.

Subscriptions

MemoMind Unlimited subscriptions are processed by Apple through the App Store. MemoMind does not receive or store your payment card information.
