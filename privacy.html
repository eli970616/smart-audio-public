MemoMind Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 12, 2026

Overview

MemoMind is designed for private voice notes. The app records voice memos and creates transcripts, summaries, action items, and reminders on device.

Data Collection

MemoMind does not collect personal data, audio recordings, transcripts, summaries, or action items on a developer-operated server.

On-Device Processing

Audio transcription and note generation are processed on device. Your saved memos remain on your device unless you choose to share them using iOS system features.

Model Downloads

MemoMind may download on-device AI model files needed for transcription and summarization. These downloads are used to enable local processing.

Subscriptions

MemoMind Unlimited subscriptions are processed by Apple through the App Store. MemoMind does not receive or store your payment card information.

Contact

If you have privacy questions, email elidrinkscoffee@gmail.com.
